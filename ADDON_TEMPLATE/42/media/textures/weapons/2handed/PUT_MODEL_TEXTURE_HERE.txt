MODEL TEXTURE REQUIREMENTS
==========================

Place your cart's texture PNG file here, named to match models_mycartaddon.txt.

For the example cart, create:
  mycart.png

Specifications:
- Size: 512x512 or 1024x1024 pixels
- Format: PNG (transparency optional)
- UV Layout: Must match your FBX model's UV unwrap

This texture is applied to:
- The 3D model when held by player
- The 3D model when placed on ground
